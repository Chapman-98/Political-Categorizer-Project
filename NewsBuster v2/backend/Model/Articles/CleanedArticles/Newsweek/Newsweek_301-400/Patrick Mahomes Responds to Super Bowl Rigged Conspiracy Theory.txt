Patrick Mahomes has responded to the conspiracy theory that this year's Super Bowl was rigged.The Kansas City Chiefs' quarterback appeared on CNN on Monday night, following the team's win against the San Francisco 49ers on Sunday.During an interview, anchor Abby Phillip asked Mahomes for his thoughts on an unsubstantiated rumor that has been making the rounds online.Conspiracy theorists claim that the Chiefs' victory was a fix and the game was scripted. Travis Kelce, the team's tight end, has been dating Taylor Swift since last summer, with the pop star's presence at games causing a surge in viewership and profits for the NFL.The singer is also vocal about politics, encouraging her followers to vote and supporting progressive causes such as LGBTQ rights and feminism."There were some wild conspiracy theories flying around this season that the Taylor Swift effect was also scripted to allow Taylor to use the Super Bowl for politics," Phillip told Mahomes. "What do you make of just how this has spiraled?""It's been wild," the football star replied. "I try to focus it on football just as much as possible, but there's always some conspiracy theories out there."I just always try to enjoy football and my family."Newsweek has reached out to Patrick Mahomes and Taylor Swift for comment via email.This is a breaking story and will be updated.Link to Image
